Privacy Policy for Dah Settle

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Dah Settle is built with your privacy as a top priority. We believe your financial data should stay yours.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

Dah Settle does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Require you to create an account
  • Store data on remote servers
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar technologies

How Your Data is Stored

All your expense data, member information, and payment records are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it for backup purposes.

Data Export

You have full control over your data. The app includes an export feature that allows you to save your data as a backup file. This file is stored only where you choose to save it, and we never receive a copy.

No Third-Party Services

Dah Settle does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Dah Settle does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect any data, changes will likely be minimal.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [your email or support page].

Summary

Simple version: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share anything. Period.
